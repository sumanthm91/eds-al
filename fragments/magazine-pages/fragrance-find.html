THE NOTES

The scents are made from fragrance notes, which has three layers that together create what is called a fragrance pyramid.

TOP NOTES - The crush

The first impression that sets the mood of the scent.

MID NOTES - The heart

Brings the fragrance to full life and defines its true essence.

BASE NOTES - The deep

The depth that leaves a long-lasting impression.
